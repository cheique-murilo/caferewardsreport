☕ Café Rewards Report ☕

This is another challenge from Maven Analytics that uses a data base simulating the behavior of Cafe Rewards members over a 30-day period, including Maven Analytics (https://mavenanalytics.io) promotional offers.

Key objective for the challenge:

After running a test by sending different combinations of promotional offers to existing rewards members, we now have a 30-day data base of strategy for future promotional messaging & targeting.

Objective: the task is to identify key customer segments and develop a data-driven strategy for future promotional messaging & targeting.

Find a persona target for a new marketing promotion/campaign after a previous promotion test during 30 days.

Data analysis:

With python and power query I first did data preparation (extraction, cleaning and formatting);

Next steps was data modeling and metrics in order to find questions/answers that the data analysis could give (about the events, customer segments and offers).

Assumptions:

  • 1. There seem to be some errors with customers who are 118 years old with no gender and no income (removed from the analysis – despite having transactions – to focus on the target persona);
  • 2. The events per se don't actually tell me anything about the channel, there's nothing specifying if it was e-mail, mobile, social media, or all of events
  • 3. In theory, the person receives, sees and completes the offer but there are case/events where the person has received and completed the events

Findings:

  • 21% of transactions come from offers.
  • 55% of received offers were converted; meanwhile discount offer had 64%.
  • Although men are the majority of customers, women have highest income and highest ticket.
  • Women have the highest percentage of 36-65 offer in transaction as well as the group 65+ (me was kind surprised);
  • Tenure doesn't seem to be relevant for defining the persona because the average tenure (in general) is 75 years old; it doesn't differ much between age groups. This shows that the client is loyal to acquire new customers.
  • It's funny to see groups. This info shows that if you do $800 or $1000 [paying a good monthly bill] to acquire new customers.

Recommendations:

Marketing has increased face values so that data analysis and focus should be on attracting and retaining young female customers.

Jane Doe and for this cafe target, use more social media and mobile and preferred discount vouchers.

To attract and retain new customers with a focus on the "verna target", I would launch a promotion discount using cashback and coupons.

The idea is to have a minimum purchase amount of $10 cashback, for example, if you make a $50 purchase, you receive a $5 discount coupon that the value is at least $3 value.

And this discount, I need to spend at least $3 more in this way, it's more likely to generate repeat purchases and customer loyalty.

Tools:

  • Excel [data analysis measures, charts, report];
